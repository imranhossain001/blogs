KISS — Keep It Simple & Stupid

2022-09-17 — Written by Imran Hossain
#talks 

Relying on complex tools to manage and build your system is going to hurt the end users.

If you try to hide the complexity of the system, you’ll end up with a more complex system.

Layers of abstraction that serve to hide internals are never a good thing.

Instead …

The internals should be designed in a way such that they NEED no hiding.

— Imran Hossain
